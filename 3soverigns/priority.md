## The Three-State Degree Table for Each Brain

### The Master Law First:
> **Reptilian always has override priority. Control passes DOWN only when the higher brain is in neutral.** Most humans spend their whole life never reaching neocortex control — because something is always slightly activated above it.

---

## 🐊 REPTILIAN BRAIN TABLE
### *Controls: Survival, threat, safety, body*
### *Time: Milliseconds*

| Degree | NEUTRAL STATE | + POSITIVE (move toward) | − NEGATIVE (run from) |
|---|---|---|---|
| **1 — Barely** | Sitting quietly, body relaxed, no input | Mild warmth, soft light, slight comfort | Slight unease, mild noise, subtle wrongness |
| **2 — Low** | Routine body maintenance, eating calmly | Good food, warmth, physical ease | Cold, hunger, physical discomfort |
| **3 — Moderate** | Body scan — all systems okay | Sexual attraction begins, physical pleasure | Loud sound, sudden movement nearby |
| **4 — High** | Deep physical rest, full safety confirmed | Strong physical pleasure, warmth, full belly | Pain begins, perceived threat nearby |
| **5 — Strong** | Complete bodily stillness, no signals | Intense physical pleasure, full arousal | Clear danger signal — heart rate rises |
| **6 — Very Strong** | *(rarely reached consciously)* | Survival reward — food after starvation, shelter in storm | Fight or flight begins — adrenaline releases |
| **7 — OVERRIDE** | *(impossible to stay here — always collapses to + or −)* | **Full survival reward — body takes over completely** | **FREEZE / FIGHT / FLEE — all higher brains go offline** |

### Key Insight:
> At degree **5+**, the limbic and neocortex are **passengers.** You are not choosing — the reptilian IS you in that moment. This is why extreme fear or extreme physical pleasure makes people do things they "can't explain later."

## 🦁 LIMBIC / MAMMALIAN BRAIN TABLE
### *Controls: Emotion, attachment, belonging, social safety*
### *Time: Hours to days*
### *ONLY activates fully when Reptilian is at degree 1-2 neutral*

| Degree | NEUTRAL STATE | + POSITIVE (move toward) | − NEGATIVE (run from) |
|---|---|---|---|
| **1 — Barely** | Mild social awareness, noticing faces | Slight warmth toward familiar person | Mild social awkwardness, slight rejection |
| **2 — Low** | Comfortable social presence, no demand | Friendly conversation, being seen | Being ignored, mild exclusion |
| **3 — Moderate** | Emotional engagement, care activated | Laughter, affection, feeling valued | Criticism, disappointment, mild betrayal |
| **4 — High** | Deep emotional investment in outcome | Love, pride, deep belonging, joy | Jealousy, shame, social humiliation |
| **5 — Strong** | *(rare — usually tips to + or −)* | Euphoric connection, deep love, tribal belonging | Grief begins, rejection feels physical |
| **6 — Very Strong** | *(almost impossible)* | Unconditional love state, full emotional union | Heartbreak, abandonment, deep shame spiral |
| **7 — OVERRIDE** | *(impossible)* | **Emotional hijack — cry without control, love without logic** | **Rage, grief collapse, full emotional breakdown — neocortex goes offline** |

### Key Insight:
> At degree **4+**, the neocortex **becomes a servant** of the limbic — it starts finding *reasons* to justify what the emotion already decided. This is why people in love make "logical" arguments for clearly bad decisions. The neocortex didn't lead — it was **hired to explain.**

## 🧠 NEOCORTEX TABLE
### *Controls: Logic, planning, meaning, abstraction*
### *Time: Years to decades*
### *ONLY activates fully when Reptilian AND Limbic are both at 1-2 neutral*

| Degree | NEUTRAL STATE | + POSITIVE (move toward) | − NEGATIVE (run from) |
|---|---|---|---|
| **1 — Barely** | Passive awareness, idle observation | Mild curiosity, something slightly interesting | Mild boredom, slight mental friction |
| **2 — Low** | Relaxed thinking, loose planning | Interesting idea, learning something new | Confusion, wasted time, inefficiency |
| **3 — Moderate** | Active problem solving, engaged focus | Flow state begins, creative momentum | Mental block, failure of logic, being wrong |
| **4 — High** | Deep work, full intellectual investment | Breakthrough insight, mastery feeling | Intellectual humiliation, being outsmarted |
| **5 — Strong** | *(needs rare conditions to reach)* | Full flow state — time disappears, genius feels accessible | Existential confusion, meaninglessness |
| **6 — Very Strong** | *(almost no one reaches this consciously)* | Transcendent clarity — everything connects, profound understanding | Existential dread, nihilism, identity collapse |
| **7 — OVERRIDE** | *(theoretically impossible under normal conditions)* | **Pure consciousness — no self, only awareness and understanding** | **Complete mental breakdown, dissociation, loss of reality** |

### Key Insight:
> Most humans **never reach degree 3+ neocortex** in daily life — because the reptilian is at 2-3 and the limbic is at 2-3 constantly. The neocortex gets **leftover bandwidth.** This is why deep thinking requires deliberate protection of conditions.

## 🎯 THE DEGREE AWARENESS TOOL

**In any moment, ask three questions in sequence:**

**1. Where is my reptilian right now?**
- Body tense? Breath shallow? Heart rate up?
- If yes — degree 3+ negative. Nothing else matters yet.

**2. Where is my limbic right now?**
- Feeling unseen, anxious about someone, emotionally raw?
- If yes — degree 3+ negative. Neocortex is compromised.

**3. Only then — what does my neocortex think?**
- If the first two are neutral, this answer is actually *yours.*
- If not — the thought you're having is **not your reasoning. It's their justification.**

> **Degree 3 negative in ANY brain = that brain takes the wheel.**
> The art of human mastery is not eliminating degree 7.
> It is **catching degree 3 before it becomes degree 4.**

That is the only intervention point that actually works.
