# Chapter 1 — Curiosity Engineering

## Problem

People leave.

People get bored.

People stop watching.

---

## What Masters Do

They create an information gap.

Brain notices:

> Something is missing.

The brain wants closure.

Examples:

* Who is the Titan?
* What is One Piece?
* Who killed Kira?
* Why did this character change?

The audience pays attention to close the gap.

---

## Formula

Known + Unknown = Curiosity

Too much known → boring.

Too much unknown → confusing.

Perfect gap → addictive.

---

### Capability Unlocked
