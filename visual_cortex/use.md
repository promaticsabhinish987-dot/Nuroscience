# Why Anime Uses Still Frames

New viewers often ask:

> Why are anime scenes sometimes static?

Because motion is expensive.

A director can save motion for critical moments.

---

Example:

```text
10 seconds still
0.2 second attack
```

Feels faster than:

```text
Continuous movement
```

because the visual cortex reacts to change.

# Technique 4: Visual Cortex Loves Edges

Remember:

V1 neurons detect edges.

Anime art exaggerates edges.

---

Real face:

```text

```

Anime face:

```text

```

The brain doesn't need all information.

It needs:


* Minimal shading
